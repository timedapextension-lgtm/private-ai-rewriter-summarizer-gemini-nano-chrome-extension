1. Information We Collect

We collect basic usage analytics to improve the extension. No personal information or text content is collected.

Important: All text processing is performed locally on your device using Google Chrome's built-in Gemini Nano AI model.

2. How We Use Information

Basic analytics help us improve extension performance and user experience. We do not sell or share this information.

3. AI Text Processing

All AI processing happens locally on your device using Google Chrome's Gemini Nano model. According to Google's implementation:

  • Your text is processed entirely on your device
  • No text content is sent to any servers
  • The AI model runs locally within your browser
  • No conversation history is stored permanently

üìç Google Chrome's Gemini Nano is designed to process text locally on your device. We leverage this Google technology to ensure your privacy.

4. Third-Party Services

The extension uses:

  • Google Chrome's Gemini Nano: AI text processing runs locally on your device as designed by Google
  • Google Analytics: Basic usage statistics to improve the extension

5. Data Security

Your text content never leaves your device. All AI processing happens locally through Google Chrome's implementation of Gemini Nano.

6. Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To interact with the current webpage for text selection features
  • host_permissions: To provide text processing overlay on all websites

These permissions are used solely to provide the extension's functionality and are not used to collect personal information.
