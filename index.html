Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 28, 2025

1. Information We Collect

The AI Summarizer extension collects minimal usage analytics to help us improve the extension experience. We collect basic information such as:

  • Extension usage frequency and feature utilization
  • General performance metrics and error reports
  • Browser version and extension version for compatibility

Important: Your actual text content is never collected, stored, or transmitted. All text processing is performed locally on your device using Google Chrome's built-in Gemini Nano AI model.

2. How We Use Information

The basic analytics we collect are used solely to:

  • Improve extension performance and user experience
  • Identify and fix bugs or compatibility issues
  • Understand which features are most valuable to users

We do not sell, share, or use this information for advertising purposes.

3. AI Text Processing

All AI processing happens locally on your device using Google Chrome's Gemini Nano model. According to Google's implementation:

  • Your text is processed entirely on your device
  • No text content is sent to Google's servers or any external servers
  • The AI model runs locally within your browser
  • No conversation history is stored permanently by our extension

4. Third-Party Services

The extension uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Chrome's Gemini Nano: AI text processing runs locally on your device as designed by Google. No text data is transmitted to Google through this AI model.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to collect basic usage statistics (feature usage, performance metrics) to improve the extension. This does not include your text content.

5. Data Security

Your text content benefits from the highest level of security because it never leaves your device. All AI processing happens locally through Google Chrome's implementation of Gemini Nano. Any analytics data we collect is transmitted securely and contains no personal text content.

6. Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To interact with the current webpage for text selection features
  • host_permissions: To provide text processing overlay on all websites

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Information

9. Children's Privacy

Our extension collects only basic usage analytics and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. All text processing is done locally on the user's device through Google Chrome's Gemini Nano implementation.

10. Your Rights

Since your text content is processed locally and never collected, there is no personal text data to access, modify, or delete. For the basic analytics we collect, you can contact us to request information about what data we have collected about your extension usage.
